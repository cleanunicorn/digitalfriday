About The Digital Friday

We are Digital Friday, a creative agency working for multiple brands and mixing our Digital world with our fuel: Fridays.
Friday, the most beautiful day of the week, helps us recharge our batteries, in order to become better creatives. We meet, talk, laugh, test, think, play. All of these keep the creative flame burning.
We believe in the power of the Story. Not just in the telling, but in the ability a great story has to offer.
We don’t have clients. We have friends. They’re our partners in this so-called life. What we love most about them is that they’re bold and dream to save the world or at least make a better place. They are market makers and we are the designer strategists.
